Title: AWS Bedrock Overview

AWS Bedrock is a fully managed service that provides access to high-performing foundation models (FMs) from leading AI companies including Anthropic, Amazon, Meta, Mistral, and Cohere — all through a single API.

Key capabilities:
- Access to Claude, Titan, Llama, Mistral, and Cohere models
- Knowledge Bases for automated RAG pipelines
- Agents for multi-step task automation
- Model evaluation and fine-tuning
- Guardrails for responsible AI deployment

Bedrock removes infrastructure management — no GPU clusters to provision, no model serving to configure. You pay per token consumed.

Security: All data stays within your AWS account. Models are not trained on your data by default.
